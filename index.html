About Coach Chris & Circular Roots

Coach Chris founded Circular Roots Coaching to offer a unique, non-conventional approach to fitness. We believe in building strength from the ground up, focusing on mobility, functional movement, and integrated training systems rather than just isolated muscle groups.

Our philosophy centers on sustainable, results-driven coaching. We don't just give you a plan; we teach you the principles of fitness, ensuring you build lasting habits and confidence long after your subscription ends. We focus on strength, flexibility, and overall well-being.
