About Coach Chris & Circular Roots

Coach Chris founded Circular Roots Coaching to offer a unique, non-conventional approach to fitness. We believe in building strength from the ground up, focusing on **mobility, functional movement, and integrated training systems.**

Our philosophy centers on sustainable, results-driven coaching. We don't just give you a plan; we teach you the principles of fitness, ensuring you build lasting habits and confidence long after your subscription ends.

How Online Coaching Works

Our process is a simple, 5-step partnership built for real life. This is the roadmap to get you **Fitter, Stronger, Better.**

1

Foundational Assessment & Design

Deep dive into your history, goals, and movement capacity to build a holistic plan covering training, mindset, sleep, and nutrition.

2

Custom, Progressive Program

A fully customized program, precisely tailored to your goals, available equipment, and weekly schedule for continuous safe progression.

3

Flexible Delivery & Execution

Access your program via a dedicated coaching app with video demos. Train anywhere—your plan adapts to your life, not the reverse.

4

Daily Accountability & Support

Benefit from constant communication, instant messaging, and quick video form checks to ensure you stay motivated and accountable.

5

Strategic Review & Scaling

Regular check-ins track key performance indicators, allowing us to pivot and scale your program safely for guaranteed long-term success.
